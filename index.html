Inside the mind of a master procrastinator

Very humorous presentation about how he procrastinated in college, but ends on a more serious note about not procrastinating in life.

Tags: Tim Urban, Ted, inspirational, humorous

From April 2016. He has a blog called Wait but Why. Very humorous talk with lots of colorful figures, e.g. instant gratification monkey and panic monster. 2 types of procrastination: there is a deadline, and there is not (exercising, seeing your family). Panic monster does not show up for non-deadline based situations. Shows a life calendar which does not have that many boxes and reminds people not to procrastinate on life.

The new era of positive psychology

Instead of only working on sick people, psychology should also help normal people be happier. Describes 3 types of happiness: pleasant, flow, and meaning.

Tags: Martin Seligman, Ted, psychology, happiness

From July 2008. The happiest people are the ones that work toward a meaningful goal, and can achieve flow. Pleasant happiness is the most superficial and shortest lasting.
